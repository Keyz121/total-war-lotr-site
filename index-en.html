About the game

Overview

Real-time battles combined with a grand campaign map of Middle-earth and massive armies.

You take control of one of the factions of Middle-earth and lead it through the War of the Ring. On the campaign map you manage economy, cities and armies, while in tactical battles you command thousands of units and legendary heroes in real time.

Key features

  • Grand campaign map of Middle-earth with key regions: Gondor, Mordor, Rohan and more.
  • Large-scale tactical battles in real time inspired by the Total War series.
  • Unique factions with their own economy, roster, playstyle and goals.
  • Heroes and commanders inspired by characters from The Lord of the Rings.
  • Story campaign following the War of the Ring and alternative "what if" scenarios.

Who is this game for

The project targets players who enjoy deep strategy with tactical battles, fans of the Total War series and The Lord of the Rings. If you ever wanted to lead Gondor against Mordor on your own campaign map, this project is made for you.

FAQ

When will Total War LotR be released?
The project is in an early stage. We’re focusing on the concept and a first prototype. More precise dates will appear here as development progresses.
Which platforms will the game be available on?
The initial target is PC. Additional platforms and formats (standalone game or mod) will be announced later.
Is this an official Lord of the Rings or Total War game?
No. Total War LotR is an unofficial fan project, not affiliated with or endorsed by SEGA, Creative Assembly, Middle-earth Enterprises or the owners of The Lord of the Rings IP. All names and universe elements are used as inspiration only.
How can I join testing?
A signup form and links to official VK and Telegram communities will appear here later. For now you can email us via the "Contact" section and mention that you’d like to be a tester.
